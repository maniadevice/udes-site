Madrasahs & Water Pump

September 2020

Alhamdulillah, we are delighted to announce the completion of the following projects in Cameroon.

  • Primary school in Mbangray
  • High school in Meinganga
  • Water pump in Mbangray

The primary school in Mbangray completed development in September 2020, will accommodate 700 students - both boys and girls. It will consist of six grades and teach both Islamic studies and academics in Arabic and French. We have also managed to complete the construction of a high school in Meiganga. This school will teach two grades and will accommodate both boys and girls.

Lastly, we also managed to complete the construction of a water pump, providing clean drinking water to the people in the village of Mbangray.

We are grateful to Allah for his grace by which this was possible. We would like to thank the Muslims in the UK by whose support these projects were funded. May the Almighty accept it from you.
