Union Dar-assalam Education et la Solidarite

UDES is a charity registered in Cameroon and United Kingdom working to help refugees fleeing persecution in Central African Republic.

Borgop Refugee Camp

The Borgop village houses one of the three UN-run camps for the refugees from Central African Republic. It accomodates nearly 14,000 people including women, elderly men and children, many of whom are orphans. The village is located at a distance of about 35 miles from the town of Meiganga (shown in the map).


Refugees live in poor conditions and are in dire need of support in terms of food, clothing, education and potentially sustainable economic means.
