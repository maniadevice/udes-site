Union Dar-assalam Education et la Solidarite

UDES is a charity registered in Cameroon and United Kingdom working to help refugees fleeing persecution in Central African Republic.

Borgop Refugee Camp

The Borgop village houses one of the three UN-run camps for the refugees from Central African Republic. It accomodates nearly 14,000 people including women, elderly men and children, many of whom are orphans. The village is located at a distance of about 35 miles from the town of Meiganga (shown in the map).


Refugees live in poor conditions and are in dire need of support in terms of food, clothing, education and potentially sustainable economic means.


To learn more about the history and what we are doing to help, click here.

What we have managed to provide so far...

Food
During the month of Ramadan we visited the camp to provide them meat, dairy and other perishable and non-perishable food items.

Clothing
We provided clothes that were collected from generous donors and also had the opportunity to provide some of the orphans with new clothes for Eid.

Relgious needs
We helped build a Masjid so that the refugees can perform their five daily prayers. A Madrasah for both girls and boys with paid teachers was also established.

Support
We try as much as possible to give the care each of them need. We did help some families to build a basic source of income, and we intend to support a few more.

We are very grateful to the excellent support provided by the charity Caravan of Mercy and their generous donors. We have a long way to go in our efforts and we sincerely ask you to support us with your sadaqah, zakah or general donations.
