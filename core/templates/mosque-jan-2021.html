Masjid Construction

January 2021

Alhamdulillah, we are pleased to announce that the construction of another Masjid in the village of Meiganga has been completed. This project took just three months for completion and will accommodate for 150 people.


We are extremely thankful for the supporters and the donors of UDES. This initiative cannot succeed without your support. May Allah reward you abundantly. If you need any further information or would like to support us, please do not hesitate to contact us.
