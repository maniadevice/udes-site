Eid ul Adha 2019

Alhamdulillah, thanks to the many generous individual and group donations, we were able to perform the qurbani and deliver the meat to the refugees in the camp. In total, we slaughtered 15 cows, 9 sheep and a goat that were used to feed 1800 families. In addition, we provided hot meals to 450 orphan children. We also distributed 190 Quran mushaf for the refugees.


We are extremely thankful for the supporters and the donors of UDES. This initiative cannot succeed without your support. May Allah reward you abundantly. If you need any further information or would like to support us, please do not hesitate to contact us.
